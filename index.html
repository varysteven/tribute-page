Arnold Schwarzenegger

A tribute to the actor, businessman, governor, bodybuilder, and inspiration.

Learn more about the wonderful life of Arnold Schwarzenegger:

  • 1947 - Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger was born in Thai, Styria on July 30th, 1947.
  • 1961 - At the age of 14, Arnold begins intensive training, which began his legendary career as a competitive bodybuilder.
  • 1968 - Arnold Schwarzenegger moves to Los Angeles, California at the age of 21 after realizing his dream to move to the U.S.A.
  • 1970 - Arnold becomes the youngest ever Mr. Olympia in the world at age 23, a title he still holds today.
  • 1982 - Arnold stars in his first box office hit film, Conan the Barbarian, marking the start of his career as an action superstar.
  • 2003 - On October 7, 2003, Arnold becomes the Governor of California after Gray Davis is removed from office in a recall election.
